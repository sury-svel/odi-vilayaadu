Privacy Policy for Odi Vilayaadu

Effective Date: May 14, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect account and registration data, including:

  • Parent and volunteer info (email, name)
  • Child info (name, age, gender, event participation)
  • Push notification tokens
  • Non-sensitive device info

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data to register participants, show schedules and scores, and communicate via push notifications. We never sell or share your data for advertising.

3. Data Security

All data is stored securely using our backend (Supabase) and is encrypted in transit. Access is restricted to event organizers only.

4. Children’s Privacy

Children’s profiles are created by parents. We do not allow self-registration by minors. Contact us to remove any data entered without consent.

5. Your Rights

  • Update or delete your profile
  • Remove child data
  • Opt-out of push notifications

Email us at support@amchats.org to request any of the above.

6. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy and will reflect the date above. Continued app use after changes means you accept the new terms.

7. Contact

For any questions, contact us at:
